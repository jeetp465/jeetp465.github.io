About

I'm a graduate student pursuing Masters in Business Analytics from University of California, Davis. I firmly believe that in this data-driven age, we can unlock the latent patterns that lies within the humongous amount of data we generate to make better decisions. I graduated from IIT Kanpur in 2017 with a bachelor's degree in Computer Science and Engineering. Along the way, I also happened to obtain a Minor in Industrial Management and Engineering.

Outside of work, you can find me enjoying a hearty game of Snooker or Foosball. Pretty decent at it. Occasionally, I also indugle in playing board games like Settlers of Catan and Codenames. Besides these, I love traveling and exploring new places, which is further fueled by my love of capturing the world through my own perspective.
